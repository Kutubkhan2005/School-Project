Digital Property Rights(DPR)

Anything created in digital form is termed as a digital property. It may exist either on an electronic storage device or online including the informationnecessary to access the digital propery.

The digital property rights include contractual right. For example, Internet accounts are governed by contracts betweeen an individual and a service provider of various accounts such as e-mail account, a shopping website account, a blog hosting account, a social networking account, an online game, and an online bank service account.

Digital property rights also include intellectual property right. For example, he digital property or digital properties such as pictures, music, movies, literaly works, webpages, and other works include intellectual property rights also.
