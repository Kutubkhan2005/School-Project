E-COMMERCE

Ecommerce or Electronic commerce is an online system to conduct commercial transaction over the Internet. It is modern way of doing buisness using the Internet. In recent years, e-commerce has grown exponentially in India. Let us touch upon a few issues concerning e-commerce.

Advantages of E-commmerce

  • It is available 24 x 7 around the year.
  • It is easier to start an e-commerce venture, as setup costs are relatively low.
  • It offers better service to customers, because distance is no hindrance.
  • Customers can select a product from a wide range of chices from different providers
  • The process of buying and selling is relatively fast.
  • It greatly reduces the buisness running cost.
  • Customers are drawn to E-commerce for the convinience of shopping from home.

Drawbacks of E-commerce

  • Many people are still concerned about data security and the possibilty of fraud. They hesitate to enter sensitive info such as credit numbers on website. Companies are working to overcome this issues.
  • Customers also hesitate to puchase online as they cannot experience the product physically. They doubt whether the product will match thier expactation.
