Spyware

The term Spyware refers to a software that secretly gathers and transmit information about users, their computer and/or their web browsing habits. This information is mostly used for advertising. Spyware may also harm a user's computer by installing malicious software and changing the computer's settings in a manner that adversely affests its performance.

Phising

It is a form of internet fraud that aims to trick people in revealing sensitive information such as their credit card numbers and passwords. THis is accomplished either through a forged e-mails appearing to have come from a genuine source or through websites that claim and appear to be well-known organizations. Damage caused by phising may range from blocked access to one's e-mail account to severe financial losses.
