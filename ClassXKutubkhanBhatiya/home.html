Introduction to Cyberethics

In the present digital world, people have the freedom to express themselves in a variety of ways using a wide range of communication technologies. The excessively high degree of connectivity, availabe through social networks, make a message, whether right or wrong, spread all over in no time. The World Wide Web stores umpteem amount of data on every topic we can possible think of. However, the correctness and authenticity of the data cannot be ascertained always. Obviously, all this freedom come along with comparable degree of risks.

     

What is Cyberethics?

The negative aspects of the modern communication systems based on modern information and modern communication technologies must be taken into account for its meaningful use. The users must use these systems responsibly so that the other users are not harmed in any way. Cyberethics are the moral principles that govern a person's behaviour in conducting of an activity related to the modern information and modern communication systems. This chapter website gives information focusing on the ethical issues relevant to the modern computer and communication systems.
