Freedom Of Information

The freedom of information comes with the endorsement of the United Nation's Universal Declaration of Human Rights. It includes freedom of opinion and freedom of acess to information which means to right seek, receive, and impart inforamtion and ideas through any medium such as books, magazines, social media, websites.

As Internet is very popular, people are comfortable wiht social media websites such as facebook and Linkedin to express their views.

Governments across the world are also using digital technology for information and service deleivers because it is fast, has no geographic limitaions, is affordable,and requires less manpower.
